Pages:
 - product entry: allows the addition of new products and/or new stock for existing products
 - product sale: allows the sale of existing stock; gets updated in real-time
 - product inventory: shows the paginated list of existing stock, updated in real-time
 - edit company details
 - show "todos" (when entering a new purchase / sale order, if the company is new just create it and add the company name to the "todo" list)

All those pages require user authentication.
